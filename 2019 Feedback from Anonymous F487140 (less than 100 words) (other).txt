
There are 2 trust services that are not included in Eidas and should:

1. Paper documents digitization. Composed of: image capture, esignature or eseal, and edocument preservation. Optional download link
inclusion.
2. Digital Handwritten Electronic Signature. Composed of: Advanced signature, biometric signature capture, biometric information
encryption, document hash binding, Comparison with reference signature

Video onboarding defined in article 24 and related to article 8 should be harmonized among countries and defined in common requirements.
These requirements should be developed in an ETSI standard. Video onboarding services should be defined as independent qualified
services.
